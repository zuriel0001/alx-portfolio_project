Rock Paper Scissors

About the Game About Us

This is a classic game where players choose between rock, paper, or scissors, and the winner is determined based on the choices made.

Our home page has a description of the game and how it could be played. The game rules are also on the home page as well as the options to choose which opponent to play with. Either against the computer or against a friend.

When you make a choice, the computer does same at the same time. Then the rules are applied and the winnter determined. The scores are updated after each round and the final winner is displayed in a popup at the end of the 5th round. You can always RESET and play again

To play with a friend, you must shaer a unique game link with them and both parties must open the link in thier browsers. To get the link, click on COPY LINK, thi command generates and copy the link at the same time.
