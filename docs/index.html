About the Game

Rock Paper Scissors - A Classic Showdown Welcome to the classic game of Rock Paper Scissors! It's a timeless battle of wits and strategy. Players face off by choosing between three options: Rock, Paper, or Scissors. The winner is determined by the rules governing the interactions between these choices. How to Play Homepage: Explore the home page for an introduction to the game and instructions on how to play. Choose your opponent: Computer or a friend. Against Computer: Make your move, and the computer will respond simultaneously. Scores are updated after each round, and the final winner is revealed after five rounds. Reset and play again for endless fun! Against a Friend: Share a unique game link with your friend? Both players open the link in their browsers.
